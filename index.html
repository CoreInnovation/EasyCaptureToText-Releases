Questions

Is it free?

You get 14 days of everything, no account needed. After that it stays free forever with a limit of 10 captures a day. Want no limits? A one-time $9.99 unlocks unlimited captures for good — no subscription, ever.

Does it work offline?

Completely. It uses the OCR engine built into Windows, so there is no internet, no account, and no data leaving your PC. Your captures are never sent anywhere.

Windows said "Windows protected your PC"?

That is SmartScreen being cautious with a new, unsigned app. Click More info, then Run anyway. The app installs per-user (no admin prompt) and uninstalls cleanly from Settings → Apps.

Does it connect to the internet at all?

Only to check for updates: an anonymous version check you can switch off in Settings. It never carries anything you captured.

What do I need?

Windows 10 or 11 (x64). That is it — the download bundles everything else.
